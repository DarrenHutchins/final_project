What do we recycle?

We'll take Old:

  • Dishes
  • Boxes
  • Routers
  • Cables

What will we do?

Our experienced engineers will visit your property and remove any old Sky equipment you have.

We will take your old dish off the wall, fill in screw holes (if any). Remove the old cable and take them all completely away!

Satelite dishes and Cables

Satelite dishes are primarily made up of:

  • Plastic
  • Metal
  • Circuit Board

Most recyclers will accept Satellite dishes as scrap metal and the plastic will be removed as part of the recycling process.
The circuit board, which contains lead, mercury, cadmium; will be extracted from the dish and recycled separately.

Copper and aluminium are used in cables, leads and wiring. These are materials that can be turned into new products.
Recycling these also means the plastic coating won’t end up polluting the environment.

Boxes and Routers

We can reuse most parts of your old products, and any parts we can’t reuse we recycle.

We aim to minimise the impact of waste on the environment, by reducing the amount going to landfill and increasing reuse and recycling of old products.
By collecting waste electrical and electronic products separately, we can ensure they are sent for specialist treatment and assessment for reuse or recycling.
