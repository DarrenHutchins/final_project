Then this is for you!
Okay, you want to get rid of a Satellite dish that is attached to your home.
Old boxes stuck in your loft?
What do you do?
Short answer is Log onto SkyUpCycle.co.uk

Booked an engineer visit to recycle old equipment? Here’s everything you need to know.
Our experienced engineers will visit your property, Someone over 18 (who can approve any work needed) will need to be home for the full time slot.
They will do a quick survey to see what needs to be removed.

We will take your old dish off the wall, fill in screw holes, remove cable and so much more.
We will even get up in the loft to get that 20-year-old box you were keeping 'JUST IN CASE'.
We will then pack away, taking anything we have removed with us leaving your property as if it were our own home.

Dishes
Most recyclers will accept Satellite dishes as scrap metal<
The plastic from the LNB will be removed as part of the recycling process. The circuit board, which contains lead, mercury, cadmium; will be extracted from the dish and recycled separately.
Cable
Copper and aluminium are used in cables, leads and wiring. These are materials that can be turned into new products.
Recycling these also means the plastic coating won’t end up polluting the environment.
Boxes & Routers
We can reuse most parts of your old products, and any parts we can’t reuse we recycle.
