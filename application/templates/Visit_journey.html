Visit Journey

Booked an engineer visit to recycle old equipment? Here’s everything you need to know.

On the day of your visit

An engineer will visit your home within the confirmed time slot we’ve given you.
When they’re on their way, you’ll get a text with an expected time of arrival.
Someone over 18 (who can approve any work needed) will need to be home for the full time slot.

Your visit details

Sign in to track your order, from there you can check:

  • Visit time and date
  • Reschedule visit

What happens next

Your engineer will arrive within the time slot we gave you for your visit.
The visit may have been missed by you or the engineer if the visit slot has passed.
If this happens,We will call you to rebook the visit for the earliest possible date.
If at any point you need to reschedule your visit time or date;
We won't be able to bring forward, but we can reschedule for a future date.
